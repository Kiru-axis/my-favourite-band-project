The band was formed in 1996 by three highschool friends: Mike Shinoda, Brad Delson and Rob Bourdon. They recruited Mark Wakefield as their lead vocalist and called the band Xero.Lack of opportunities prompted Wakefield to leave and was replaced by Chester Bennington. They decided to change the band's name to Hybrid Theory bur later changed it to Linkin Park, to avoid confusion with Hybrid, another British band.
